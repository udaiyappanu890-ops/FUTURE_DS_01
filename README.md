Business Sales Analysis Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing business sales data to uncover revenue trends, top-performing products, high-value categories, and regional performance insights.

The dashboard provides a client-ready analytical view of sales performance and supports data-driven decision-making to improve profitability and growth strategy.

🎯 Project Objectives

Analyze overall revenue trends over time

Identify top-selling products

Determine high-value product categories

Evaluate regional sales performance

Generate actionable business insights

📈 Key KPIs Analyzed

Total Revenue

Total Orders

Total Quantity Sold

Average Order Value (AOV)

Monthly/Yearly Revenue Growth

Top Products by Revenue

Category-wise Revenue Contribution

Region-wise Sales Performance

🔎 Key Insights

Revenue shows seasonal trends with peak sales in high-demand periods.

A small percentage of products contribute to the majority of total revenue.

Certain categories consistently outperform others in profitability.

Regional performance varies significantly, indicating opportunities for targeted marketing.

High-revenue regions can be leveraged for expansion strategies.

📊 Dashboard Features

Revenue trend analysis (monthly/yearly)

Top 10 products by revenue

Category performance comparison

Regional sales heatmap or comparison chart

KPI summary cards for executive overview

Interactive filters (date, category, region)

🛠 Tools & Technologies Used

Power BI / Tableau / Excel / Python

Data Cleaning & Transformation

Data Modeling

KPI & Business Metrics Calculation

Interactive Data Visualization

💡 Business Value

This analysis helps stakeholders:

Identify revenue growth opportunities

Make informed budgeting and forecasting decisions
